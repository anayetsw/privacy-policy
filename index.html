Privacy Policy

This application uses Firebase, a platform provided by Google, to collect anonymous information about how the app is used. This information helps us understand user interactions and improve functionality.

Data We Collect

  • Usage frequency
  • Crashes and errors (Crashlytics)
  • General device information (model, operating system version)
  • In-app events (features used, time spent in the app)

Purpose of Data Collection

The collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Analyze app performance
  • Identify and fix bugs
  • Improve user experience

Data Sharing

We do not share the collected data with third parties. The data is processed by Google through Firebase in accordance with their privacy policy.

Security

We apply appropriate technical measures to protect the collected information.
